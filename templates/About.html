About

  • The main aim of this project is to predict the chances of being affected by liver disease at a early stage and bring awareness.
  • The results of blood test report are entered in the fields provided and a click on predict button will give the result .
  • The output is displayed whether the individual have liver disease or not helps to alert the individual about potential problems such as liver cancer,fatty liver disease,hepatitis,Jaundice,Diarrhea etc.
  • The Enzymes levels of ALT,AST,ALP,Albumin and total protiens,Direct and Total Bilirubin are the basis for the prediction.
  • The ML model random forest classifier is used for prediction with indian_liver_patients CSV file as dataset.
